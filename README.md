## 🌟 Features

### 🎯 **AI-Powered Mood Interpretation**
- **Text-to-Visual AI** - Describe your mood and watch the landscape transform
- **Intelligent keyword mapping** - Detects emotions like "neon", "peaceful", "volcanic", "cosmic"
- **Smooth transitions** - Seamless morphing between visual states

### 🎵 **Real-Time Audio Reactivity**
- **Microphone integration** - Your voice and music drive the visualization
- **Bass frequency mapping** - Deep sounds create dramatic terrain peaks
- **Treble frequency mapping** - High frequencies control particle glow and speed
- **Ambient mode** - Beautiful motion even without microphone input

### 🎨 **Stunning Visual Effects**
- **Glass-morphism UI** - Modern, transparent interface with hover effects
- **UnrealBloomPass** - Cinematic bloom and glow effects
- **Dynamic particle systems** - Thousands of reactive particles
- **Floating orbs** - Ethereal spheres that pulse with audio
- **Real-time terrain morphing** - Mountains and valleys that dance to sound

### 🎮 **Interactive Controls**
- **Auto-rotating camera** - Cinematic orbiting view
- **Mouse-controlled UI** - Interface appears on movement
- **Manual orbit controls** - Click and drag to explore
- **Responsive design** - Works on desktop and mobile
